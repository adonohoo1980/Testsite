FAQ

Quick answers to the stuff everyone asks.

Do you charge a fee?

No. We’re paid by the carrier if you place coverage. Quotes are always free.

Can you match my current coverages?

Yes — and we’ll also show a “better protection” option so you can compare.

How hard is it to switch?

Easy. We set an effective date, handle electronic signatures, and help coordinate cancellation if needed.

Do you only work with one insurance company?

No — we’re independent, so we can shop multiple carriers based on your needs.

Ready for a clean quote and a clear answer?

Send the basics and we’ll come back with 2–3 solid options. Then we’ll do a quick coverage walkthrough so there are no surprises.
